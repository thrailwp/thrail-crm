== Thrail CRM ==  
A powerful and easy-to-use WordPress CRM plugin for lead management, email automation, and much more!  

== Description ==  
**Thrail CRM** is a cutting-edge WordPress plugin designed to enhance your customer relationship management. Perfect for businesses looking to streamline lead generation, automate email communication, and efficiently manage their customer database.  

> **Note**: This plugin requires PHP 7.2 or higher.  

### Key Features:  
- **Opt-in Form**: Add an attractive lead capture form using the shortcode `[thrail-crm]`.  
- **Lead Management**: Effortlessly store and manage leads from a user-friendly admin interface.  
- **AJAX-Powered Operations**: Quickly edit, update, or delete leads without page reloads.  
- **Email Automation**: Send emails instantly or schedule them to send within an hour.  
- **Email Logs**: Maintain a detailed record of all sent emails for reference.  
- **CSV Export**: Export leads with custom filters for streamlined reporting and analysis.

== Installation ==  
1. Download the plugin and upload the `thrail-crm` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.  
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** menu in WordPress.  
3. Use the shortcode `[thrail-crm]` to display the opt-in form on your site.  
4. Navigate to "Thrail CRM" in your dashboard to manage leads, emails, and logs.
